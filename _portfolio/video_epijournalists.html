--- Video course - Epidemiology and science for journalists (Spanish)

https://apcienciaperu.wordpress.com/curso-de-epidemiologia-y-ciencias-para-periodistas/

In response to the COVID-19 pandemic and infodemic, I was part of the team that developed this course about epidemiology and science for journalists, at APCIENCIA (Peruvian Association of Science Journalists and Communicators). I interviewed Dr. Jaime Miranda and Dr. Gabriel Carrasco, about basic concepts in epidemiology and their relationship to the current health crisis. I also edited the raw video into the course format.
